Blackjack Rules:

1. The goal is to get as close to 21 points as possible without exceeding it.
2. Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value.
3. Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10 points.
4. Aces can be worth 1 or 11 points, whichever is more favorable.
5. Each player starts with two cards, and can choose to "hit" (take another card) or "stand" (keep their current hand).
6. The dealer must hit until their cards total 17 or higher.
7. If a player exceeds 21 points, they "bust" and lose the game.
8. The player closest to 21 without going over wins.
